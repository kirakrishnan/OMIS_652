Business Description

Rainbow Inn

Main motive of Rainbow Inn is to provide international standards within the reach of every individual. Rainbow Inn features over 100 motels. You will find our rooms comfortable and welcoming. Our guest’s safety and security is of utmost concern to us. We provide free wireless internet service to our customers. We also have an indoor heated swimming pool and we have a complimentary breakfast in the morning from 6:00 AM to 9:00 AM.

 

We have different types of rooms. Customers can book rooms in three ways. They can either book it online through a third party site, call to the motel front desk and book a room and they can also do walk in reservation. Customers will get points every time they book a room. For every 1500 points they earn they can redeem those points for a night stay at motel. 

 

We will collect information from the customer when they come to check-in. The customer’s name, email ID, phone no, car model are saved in the database. We need to keep track of all the customer data. And we need to give discount to customers who do the reservations regularly. We have branches at multiple places. Each branch has different type of employees to perform different task.

 

Every day many people will be checking out of the rooms. The status of the room is changed to dirty. After that housekeeping employees should go and clean it. After the house keeping is done the status of the room is changed to clean so that it can be given to the customer who comes next.

 

Type of Employees:

Each branch has a Manager, receptionist and two housekeepers. So there are 400 employees in total.

Manager supervises the receptionist and housekeeping staff 

Receptionist handles all the front desk activities like greeting and checking in guests. They will answer to the guest inquiries and also answer phone calls.  

Housekeepers will clean guest rooms, lobbies, bathroom and facilities.  And they prepare vacant rooms for new guest.

Business Rules:

1.       Customer should check-out before 11:00 AM.

2.       Customer can check-in only after 3:00 PM.

3.       Employee should verify the customer ID before check-in.

4.       Cancellation should be made at least before 24 hours before check-in.

5.       All the employees should were only dress provided by motel and they should have an Identification Badge provided by motel.

6.       Employees should be trained about all the policies and procedures.

7.       Inventory must be regularly checked.

8.       Room should be checked before allocating to the customer.

9.        Customers should be able to give their feedback.
